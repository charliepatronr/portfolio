ConTEST NYC 2021

Test Masters Academy

Since Google introduced Android TV in 2014 and Apple released its tvOS in 2015, the TV apps industry is growing exponentially. This is powered by the need to provide apps for TV gadgets, especially for those devices that run under android tv gadgets, most of them located in our living room. The global Android TV market size is projected to reach USD 67,080 million by 2026. The apps for Android TV provide an increasing part of the added value of modern gadgets systems and therefore become more complex because almost every company is trying to release its own TV app as soon as possible.

I partipated as a speaker in the ConTEST 2021 edition for the Test Masters Acedmy where we developed a test automation project to illustrate the most efficient approach to test an application in the Android TV environment.

The technologies we used include Python and libraries such as: ADB shell, uiautomator, pytest, validators, requests, and flask.

It was an incredible experience to participate as a panelist in the ConTEST NYC where I had the opportunity to meet new people and learn from each of their projects. To anyone interested in participating in a future edition or has questions about my presentation please feel free to contact me.
